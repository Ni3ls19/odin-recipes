Monday: Air Fryer Pecan Crusted Trout

Air Fryer Pecan Crusted Trout

"This pecan crusted trout cooked in the air fryer is a quick and easy dinner that is on the table in 20 minutes. Serve with vegetables and rice." â€”devin

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup finely chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 (4-ounces) rainbow trout fillets, thawed if frozen, skinned if desired
  • 2 teaspoons pure maple syrup
  • lemon wedges, for serving
  1. Step 1 Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Step 2 Stir together pecans, rosemary, and salt in a shallow dish. In a second shallow dish, add flour. In a third shallow dish, lightly beat egg. Dip one side of each fillet in flour, shaking off excess; dip into egg, then into pecan mixture, lightly pressing to coat fish.
  3. Step 3 Working in batches if needed, arrange fillets on parchment paper, pecan-coating up. Transfer fish on parchment to the air fryer basket.
  4. Step 4 Cook until fish easily flakes with a fork and an instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of fillets registers 145 degrees F (62 degrees C), 7 to 8 minutes.
  5. Step 5 Drizzle fillets with maple syrup and serve with lemon wedges.
